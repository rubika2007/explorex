Samanar Hills

📍Kilakuyilkudi, Tamil Nadu 625019

🕢 Timings: 6:00 AM - 9:30 PM

🌤 Weather: Sunny, 33°C

Samanar Hills, also known as Samanar Malai or Amanarmalai or Melmalai, is a rocky stretch of hills located near Vadivelkari village, 10 kilometres west of Madurai city, Tamil Nadu, India. They stretch east–west over 3 kilometers towards Muthupatti village.
