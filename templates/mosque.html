Goripalaym Dargah

Goripalayam Dargah

📍 Pallivasal Street, Goripalayam, Madurai

🕢 Timings: 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM

🌤 Weather: Sunny, 33°C

The Goripalayam Dargah (also known as the Hazrat Sultan Sikandhar Badusha Shaheed Dargah) is the largest mosque in Madurai. Located on the northern banks of the River Vaigai, it houses the tombs of 13th-century Muslim saints and medieval rulers of the region.
